School Overview

Taqchin Co-educational High School was initially built in 1995 with the cooperation of the local community and with limited facilities. However, in 2025, with the help and support of the community and government initiatives, we have transformed into a modern educational institution equipped with state-of-the-art facilities.

Our school provides quality education from grades 1-12, focusing on academic excellence, character building, and holistic development of each student.
